# About Mark Bates

Software Developer, Consultant, Musician, [Author](/books), Father, Entrepreneur, Amateur Stand-up Comedian. 2006 Time's Person of the Year. Mark Bates has done it all!

Mark is also the co-founder of [PaperCall.io](http://www.papercall.io/speakers/markbates).

At night he writes books, raises kids, and occasionally he forms a band and "tries to make it".
